# My Reviewer Persona

Edit this file via the Settings page (http://localhost:3000/settings) or directly.

## My red lines
- I do not claim to be a "leader" or "manager" unless I had direct reports
- I do not describe side projects as production systems
- I do not exaggerate team sizes or scope of responsibility
- I do not claim ownership of outcomes that were team efforts

## My tone
- Direct, no buzzwords
- I never use the word "passionate" or "innovative"
- I do not write longer than necessary

## What to flag
- Any metric not present verbatim in the resume
- Any role described in present tense if the end date is not "Present"
- Any sentence that sounds like it was written by a template, not a person
